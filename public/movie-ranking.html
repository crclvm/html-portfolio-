The Best Movies According to Luis Helo

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Inglourious Basterds

Directed by Quentin Tarantino, some of the actors are Brad Pitt, Christoph Waltz, Melanie Laurent. In the middle of the WW2 a group of american soldiers are in charge to end the War by any end.

The Lord of The Rings

A story written by J.R.R. Tolkien in the XXth century about a hopeless mission with amazing characters like elves, dwarves, mortal men, hobbits and wizards to destroy a powerful ring with evil in it.

Armageddon

A gigantic asteroid threatens the Earth. The only solution to get rid of the threat is to send a team of astronauts into space to destroy the meteorite. NASA contacts Harry Stamper, an expert in oil drilling, so that he and his team can land on the surface of the asteroid, drill it and introduce a device that changes its trajectory when it explodes.
